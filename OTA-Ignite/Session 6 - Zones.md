# Zones


CMP
: Current Market Price

Demand Zone
: Zone below the CMP

Supply Zone
: Zone above the CMP

Proximal Line
: Line closer to CMP

Distal Line
: Line farther from CMP

> Buy at Wholesale and Sell at Retail

All Supply and Demand Zones are made of 3 elements
1. Leg-In -> Can be Rally or Drop
2. Base
3. Leg-Out - *Explosive* Price Action
	Drop - Supply Zone
	Rally - Demand Zo

> 6 Base Candles
