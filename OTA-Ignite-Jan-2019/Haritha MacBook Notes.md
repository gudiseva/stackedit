# Definitions

ATR
: Average Tool Range
  Tool gives the volatility

DATR
: Daily ATR
  `Add Study -> Select DATR for 14 days`

#### For trade types:
- Intraday, the Stop Loss rule is 2% of DATR
- Swing Trading -> 10% of DATR
---
- Entry -> Proximal Line
- Stop Loss -> Below Distal Line (- 2% DATR)
- Target -> Distal of Opposite Zone
